The Department

Production Planning sits at the crossroads of the plant — connecting the CTO and CMPO, orchestrating daily production, and ensuring every tire reaches the finish line on time.

CTO–CMPO Bridge

  • Stakeholder Monthly Production Ticket
  • Raise schedule constraints (container, machine capability, mold, etc.)
  • SPS and Balance Teams member

Production Inspector

  • Monitor aggregate subproduct inventories, plans & attainments
  • Organize CTO daily attainment meetings across production, technology, maintenance & quality
  • Report unreasonable situations at weekly plant follow-up & daily asakai meetings
  • Trigger crisis meetings & align CTO
  • Monthly attainment report

Production Planner

  • Prepare production plans for mixer, stock prep, extruder, TAM & curing
  • Daily subproduct inventories
  • Report daily machine-based plan delay reasons
  • Purchase local curing bladder
  • Prepare SAP business card

Orchestra Member

  • Control code-based plan-attainments
  • Trigger production & planners for code-based quantities and deadlines
  • Prepare monthly curing plan for SPS, MRP, etc.

The Role

Data Scientist

  • Build PowerBI reports with Oracle queries for routine monitoring

Business Development Specialist

  • Lead the digitalization of Production Planning
  • Build systems that shorten work processes, reduce error margins & decrease human dependency

What I’ve Built

Since joining Production Planning, I’ve automated core workflows and launched digitalization initiatives — here’s what’s been delivered so far.

🔧 Automation of Standard Tasks

Monthly Attainment Report

Transformed a time-consuming, error-prone manual report into an Oracle-driven automated process — delivering faster, more accurate monthly production performance summaries with minimal human intervention.

Ticket Tonnage Calculations

Automated tonnage calculations that previously required extensive manual effort — enabling rapid, error-free outputs and reducing dependency on any single role.

KPI Entry Tasks

🚀 Digitalization Initiatives

Monthly → Daily Ticket Planning

Shifted ticket planning from a monthly cycle to a daily cadence using Oracle — introducing a live KPI Dashboard and 2C view that removed manual tracking and reduced curing planner dependency.

Extruder Lot Size Planning

Optimized extruder lot sizing by leveraging Icron for longer production runs — reducing work area disruptions and enabling rapid schedule updates through Oracle.

Production Planning PowerBI Portfolio

Built a PowerBI portfolio featuring Plan-Attainment Monitor and Monthly Lot Size & Mix dashboards — giving decision-makers real-time visibility into production performance.
